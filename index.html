Erdős Problem 866: the eventual value of h₄

For Erdős Problem 866, Demonstrandum proves that h₄(n) = 4 for all n ≥ 331,777 — determining the eventual value of the constant shown to exist by Choi, Erdős, and Szemerédi in 1975. The Lean 4 kernel checks the theorem; the paper, Lean development, and complete certificate archive are public.
