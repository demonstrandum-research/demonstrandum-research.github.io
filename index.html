Latest result

Erdős Problem 866: the eventual value of h₄

Demonstrandum proves that h₄(n) = 4 for all n ≥ 331,777, determining the eventual value of the constant shown to exist in 1975 by Choi, Erdős, and Szemerédi. The Lean 4 kernel checks the proof; the paper, development, and certified-release archive are public.
