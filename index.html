Erdős Problem 866: the eventual value of h₄

Demonstrandum proves that h4(n) = 4 for all n ≥ 331,777, determining the eventual value of the constant whose existence Choi, Erdős and Szemerédi established in 1975. The theorem is checked by the Lean 4 kernel; the paper, Lean development, and full certificate archive are public.
