Wave 1: Thirteen Verified Results

Certificates: dual-checker ✓  ·  Lean results: kernel-checked ✓  ·  Statements: frozen verbatim from sources ✓  ·  External review: not yet

Feasibility study · the operation's shakedown run, comprising 13 verified results.

The operation's first release wave: eleven refutations of published conjectures, one proof of a published conjecture (Elizalde–Luo), and one numerical record — each frozen verbatim from its source before attack, and each backed by a runnable certificate accepted by independent checkers or a Lean 4 proof accepted by the kernel. Every result carries a per-result "what this does not show" caveat in the artifact record.

Limitations

These results have not been externally refereed. Refutations are finite and mechanical — the strongest kind of claim to check — but novelty statements rest on our own literature searches, whose scope is recorded per result. Statement-fidelity (that each frozen formal statement represents the cited conjecture) was reviewed internally and is documented in the artifact record.
